Naruto Uzumaki

Backstory

Naruto Uzumaki is the main protagonist of the Naruto series. He was orphaned as a baby and grew up ostracized by the villagers, as he carried the Nine-Tails Fox demon inside him. Despite this, he dreams of becoming the Hokage, the leader of his village.

Abilities

  • Master of Shadow Clone Jutsu
  • Access to Nine-Tails Chakra
  • Sage Mode
  • Rasengan & Rasenshuriken

Personality Traits

Naruto is energetic, determined, and never gives up on his friends. He is caring, brave, and has an unwavering belief in the potential of others.

First Appearance

Naruto first appeared in Naruto Chapter 1 / Episode 1.
