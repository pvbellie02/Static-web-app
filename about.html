About This Site

Welcome to the Anime Character Gallery! This website was created to showcase some of the most popular and iconic anime characters across different universes. I chose anime as the theme because I am a huge anime fan and have watched all of these shows so I know a fair amount about them. It was fun teaching people more about the characters I love.

The design choices focus on clarity and visual appeal. The dark theme and card-style layout highlight each character's image and key details, while keeping navigation consistent across the site.

Thank you for visiting! I hope you enjoy exploring these characters as much as I enjoyed putting this gallery together.
